Trip Outline

Day 1- Arrive in Hanoi Welcome to Hanoi! This is your first day in Vietnam – we will welcome you with our Yellow Hat Travels dinner tradition. Relax and breathe in the air of a country that is rich in history and tradition. Make new friends and get ready to make new memories!


Day 2- Hanoi Good morning travellers! Today, we are painting the city yellow! After breakfast, we will head to the markets. With a variety of stalls to choose from - this is the perfect time to experience the city with the locals. Get your taste buds ready to munch on some local food!


Day 3 - Halong Bay It’s time to go on an adventure! You will be transported to Halong Bay for a one night cruise. This overnight cruise will give you the opportunity to relax and enjoy the beautiful scenery around you. Floating on the waters of Halong Bay, you will get a taste of navigating between forest islands, see the vibrant and colourful fish villages. The next day, you will wake up to an absolutely stunning view!


Day 4 - Arrive in Hanoi You’re back in the city of Hanoi for one last night in this amazing city! The rest of the day is yours to enjoy at leisure. The next day we will be in Hoi An.


Day 5 - Hoi An After arriving at the airport – we will be transported to the beautiful town - Hoi An. After settling in, hit the streets and explore with us. Don’t forget that you can get tailor-made clothes at affordable prices. You want a shoe custom-made for you, a dress, a suit – well it’s your day to get your negotiating skills at work!


Day 6 - Hoi An Rise and shine like the yellow sun! Today is a day to test those cooking skills. We will enjoy a Vietnamese cooking class and bask at the product of our hands. It’s going to be a yummy experience! Afterwards, you have the rest of the afternoon to do as you please!


Day 7 - Hoi An You are on holiday – some days you just want to wake up late: this is your day. And Hoi An is perfect for that! Enjoy this day doing anything you like. Optional excursion can be arranged for those who wish to be busy on this day.


Day 8 - Da Nang After checking out from hotel we will head to Da Nang – think beach, palm trees, watersports and lots of fun!


Day 9 - Da Nang Today you will have an early hotel pick-up to get the most of this full day tour. The funicular railway will take you high up into the Ba Na hills to visit the Linh Ung pagoda, France wine cellar, Golden Bridge, flower garden, and King Peak. You will love this part of Vietnam!


Day 10 - Arrive in Ho Chi Mihn City Welcome to the energetic and vibrant city of Ho Chi Mihn. Upon arrival, take a walk through the timeless alleys and learn about your surroundings. Check out the tastiest stalls and enjoy this enchanting city.


Day 11 - Ho Mihn City Rise and shine travellers! Join us on an unforgettable Mekong Delta excursion. Let’s enjoy the stunning maze of rivers, small villages and marvellous islets. This is an unforgettable experience!


Day 12 - Ho Mihn City Today is your free day. You can explore and enjoy the day as you would like. Take advantage, shop and soak in the beautiful experience of Vietnam because you return home the next day.

Day 13 - Today we return home and reminisce on all of the fun and knowledge we’ve gained from the Vietnamese culture. Thank you for painting Vietnam yellow with us and we hope to see you again!
