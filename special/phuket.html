Trip Outline

DAY 1 - ARRIVE IN PHUKET Depending on times of arrival in Phuket, Thailand – this is a take-it-easy day after a long flight. You will enjoy time on the beach or roam the vibrant streets of this exotic island. Our welcome dinner will be at one of the beautiful restaurants by the beach.


DAY 2 - PHUKET Well good morning travellers! Today you will enjoy a full day tour to the most amazing part of the island to discover the picturesque Ao Phang Nga National Park. Explore the caves, and lagoons, and soak up unique views of the beautiful surrounding scenery.


DAY 3 - TO KOH PHI PHI
KOH PHI PHI
After breakfast we will hit the Andaman Sea in search of one very famous island - Koh Phi Phi. On arrival, we will enjoy the afternoon sipping cold island fresh juices and wondering the unique streets of Koh Phiphi. For those who wish to hike, watch the sunset over Andaman Sea from the highest viewpoint on the island.


DAY 4 - KOH PHI PHI The Phi Phi Islands Tour by Speedboat gives you a day of exploring the most stunning sights. This includes an amazingly beautiful Maya Bay, made famous by the movie The Beach, as well as Viking Cave and Monkey Beach. Afterwards – the rest of the day is at leisure.


DAY 5 - KOH PHI PHI The island is your playground and the day is yours to do whatever your heart desires. Today it’s all about swimming and chillaxing. It is a vacation afterall! In the evening, squeeze in one last beachside stroll, soaking up the island sounds and nightlife on the beach... this is what we call living!


DAY 6 - KOH PHI PHI TO KRABI The exotic paradise of Krabi is calling our names, and who are we to resist... We will hop on the boat to the next island, Krabi. This afternoon we'll soak in the small town feels of Ao Nang. Friendly locals, massage haunts, local markets and seaside cocktails can perfect your day.


DAY 7 – KRABI 4 Island Tour - We have the most unforgettable day planned for you!  Join us for an experience of a lifetime island hopping by speedboat in beautiful Krabi! This one can’t be missed!


DAY 8 – KRABI Rise and shine: today we head back to the mainland, Phuket. On arrival – do as you wish - sleep, get a massage, watch the sunset, enjoy the island cuisine – basically do whatever you please!


DAY 9 - PHUKET This is the last day in beautiful Thailand. Spend this day as you please – we will have one last time together enjoying dinner together to say goodbye to one of the most beautiful destinations on earth. You are free to do that last minute shopping for loved ones at home. As our token of appreciation – the last massage before going home is on us!


DAY 10 Today is a sad day – however you will have plenty of precious memories to share with family and friends back home.
